Crypto Artifex is an online crypto art exchange and virtual museum for all type artists

This project will empower artist by exchanging non fungible tokenized art to collectors around the world. You will be able to sell, give away and admire art from collectors and artist alike.

This project will be compleatly desentralized using Ethereum as the main network with the protocol ERC-721. Your work will become part of history. Physical art requires transportation, special care and authenticity protection which will no longer be needed when tokenizing your work. Thus becoming timeless and being preserved forever.

You just need to submit your digital work to the site in order to be verified and accepted. An optional service for physical art scanning will be available to bring your work no matter what.

Embrace the future, become a Crypto Artifex!
