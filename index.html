Crypto Artifex is an online crypto art exchange and virtual museum for all type artists

This project will empower artist by exchanging non fungible tokenized art to collectors around the world. You will be able to sell, give away and admire art from collectors and artist alike.

This project will be compleatly desentralized using Ethereum as the main network with the protocol ERC-721. Your work will become part of history as physical art which requires transportation, special care and authenticity protection wont be needed. Thus becoming timeless and being preserved forever.

You just need to submit your digital work to the site to pass a verification process to be accepted. An option for physical art 3D scanning will be available to bring your work no matter what.

Embrace the future, become a Crypto Artifex!
